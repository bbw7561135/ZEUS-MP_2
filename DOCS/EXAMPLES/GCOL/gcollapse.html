Pressureless Gravitational Collapse
Author: John Hayes


Description

A uniform sphere of unit density and initial radius of 1 cm collapses in free fall. The gravitational constant (GUNIV) was set equal to 1.0 for this test. Artificial viscosity is also zeroed (QCON=0.0). The figure below was generated from a time slice dumped at t = 0.535 sec, which is 0.985 free-fall times.
